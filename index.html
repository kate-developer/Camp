WANNA JOIN OUR
CAMPING COMMUNITY?

Welcome to our warm and friendly community! Join us, and you will receive many benefits of becoming a member! It's completely free! You will get updates about upcoming events, meetings, answers to frequent questions, updates about trails and much more! We welcome everyone! If you want to escape the city for a weekend, do not even hesitate - adventure awaits!

Every week we accept requests for the trip on the upcoming weekend. Thursday is the last day to make a request, because then we choose a place to meet and Friday after work we go on adventure which lasts till Sunday morning. It is a very friendly atmosphere and everybody is having fun and a great time.

Looking for your getaway? You've come to the right place! Join the family.
